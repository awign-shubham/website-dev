Virtual Internship: Work from Home, Get Training, Stipend & Certificate (Duration 1 Week)

What you get

  • Earn Rs 1200 in 1 Week. If you are able to showcase your skills in the job assigned, you can continue earning even more on weekly basis.
  • You will get a certificate of internship signed by the Business head after completion of the project assigned to you.
  • Top 3 performers will get an opportunity of Pre Placement Interview with the company.
  • The project needs to be executed virtually, enabling you to easily manage the internship along with your studies. You don't need to visit any office and all communication would be done over phone or skype.
  • You will be trained, guided and mentored by a team comprising of professionals from IITs and IIMs throughout the project.
  • Get direct industry exposure of how core business operations are executed.

Project Description

In this project, you will be trained and will be responsible for a very important role of lead generation and validation for a growing real-estate start-up. They provide bachelors with fully-furnished houses on sharing basis with all the necessary appliances in place. Currently, our client is operational in Bangalore and looking to expand its business through Awign.

Your job is to find potential house leads in Bangalore through various online portals and validate them as target leads for our client.

Steps to be followed

  • Find the list of relevant houses by searching through various online marketplaces. In order to ensure that only relevant leads appear in the search list, appropriate filters must be applied.
  • You need to contact the leads one by one and collect the required details to validate the house lead.
  • You would also be trained on the way you need to talk to your leads and given a set of steps that you need to follow throughout your engagement with them.
  • You would be required to maintain a database while engaging with the leads. This database would contain the details of the houses, which you will have to collect while conversing with the leads. The framework for the same would be provided.
  • You will be compensated with your stipend once the house leads are validated by us. You need to ensure that all details that you enter are verified and correct.

Key Skill Set

This internship would implore you to bring in the following skills and show them in the real world.

  • People Engagement Skills
  • Excellent Communication Skills
  • Project Management Skills
  • Problem Solving

Project Completion Guidelines

  • The stipend of Rs 1200 corresponds to successful execution of the project for 80 house leads.
  • You need to successfully bring at least 50% of the target house leads. You'll be paid on pro rata basis post this threshold.
  • Good performers will be given the opportunity to take on the responsibility of bringing in more house leads with even better stipend.
