Virtual Internship: Work from Home, Get Training, Stipend & Certificate (Duration 1 Week)

What you get

  • Earn Rs 3000 in 1 Week. If you are able to showcase your skills in the job assigned, you can continue earning even more on weekly basis.
  • You will get a certificate of internship signed by CEO of the company after completion of the project assigned to you for 1 week.
  • Top 3 performers will get an opportunity of Pre Placement Interview with the company.
  • The project needs to be executed virtually, enabling you to easily manage the internship along with your studies. You don't need to visit any office and all communication would be done over phone or skype.
  • You will be trained, guided and mentored by a team comprising of professionals from IITs and IIMs throughout the project.
  • Get direct industry exposure of how core business operations are executed.
  • Top 5 performers would get an opportunity to attend a free workshop on data analytics, conducted by data scientists passed out of IITs.

Project Description

In this project, you will be trained and will be responsible for a very important role of vendor sourcing for a growing manufacturing start-up Ally.Work. They are borne with a mission to help small and medium manufacturers grow their business. They act as sales and marketing team for manufacturing organizations, starting with Metal Fabrication and Packaging Material Manufacturing.

On their web platform, they collect verified Request for Quotation (RFQ) from brands looking for packaging suppliers. A quotation refers to a formal statement describing the estimated cost for a particular job or service. They match these RFQs to appropriate vendors and ask for quotations on the items. The quotations that come from vendors are passed to the seeker for his approval. Once cleared, the vendors can set up a meeting with the seeker and close the deal and terms of payment.

Example:

For an RFQ of manufacturing 50,000 dropper bottles (for eye drops) of 15ml for the purpose of storing deodorant, your task will be to get quotations containing the price of these bottles from vendors who deal in this sector.

Steps to be followed

  • Thoroughly understand the requirement for which the quotations have to be procured. Such a requirement is technically known as Request for Quotation (RFQ). You will be trained on every aspect of understanding the RFQ.
  • Find the list of relevant vendors by searching through appropriate keywords on various online marketplaces such as Indiamart and Tradeindia. In order to ensure that only relevant vendors appear in the search list, you must ensure that you have a good understanding of the RFQ.
  • You need to call the vendors one by one, explain the requirement to them, resolve their queries and get them to send the quotation for the RFQ. You would be trained on the way you need to talk to vendors and also given a set of steps that you need to follow throughout your engagement with the vendors.
  • You would be required to maintain a database while engaging with the vendors. The framework for the same would be provided.
  • You will be compensated with your stipend once the quotations are validated.

Key Skill Set

Engaging with correct vendors and getting the desired quotations is an art which would implore you to bring in the following skills and show them in the real world.

  • People Engagement Skills
  • Good Verbal Communication Skills
  • Leadership Skills
  • Business Awareness
  • Project Management Skills
  • Problem Solving
  • Critical Thinking

Project Completion Guidelines

  • The price of Rs 3000 corresponds to successful execution of the project for 5 RFQs and you need to get 8 quotations for each RFQ.
  • In case of unsuccessful execution of the project, the stipend will be calculated based on your performance. You will be given Rs 75 for each valid quotation that you bring, provided you bring at least 3 quotations for each RFQ.
  • Good performers will be given the opportunity to take on the responsibility of more RFQs and would be paid a better amount for each RFQ they complete.
